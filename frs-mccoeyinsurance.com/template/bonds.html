BONDS

Contractor bonding is an essential level of protection. This form of surety bond safeguards you and others from costly of unethical business decisions. A contractor’s license bonds three parties, the company that hired you, the contractor, yourself, and the state bond issuing agency involved in a contract or project. Contractor bonding guarantees that you will do no harm and always provide professional services in an ethical manner.
